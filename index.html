A day in the life

Being a University of Washington alum, I always look forward to cherry blossom season. I wish I spent more time in the quad during spring quarters, but I did make sure to walk through there often. UW is a special place and I'm thankful for all that I've learned and gained from attending this awesome university.

One of the best things was having a real husky as our mascot and it was always a good day when Dubs was spotted on campus. I even got to take a picture with him once!

"UW is the bestest school I could represent!"

-Dubs, University of Washington

I don't know how you can get any better than a school on the west coast, in a beautiful city, with a husky as a mascot, and PURPLE as the main school color. You could say college was a great experience. Take me back!
